**Performance-Based**: Wrote plays intended for performance while also valued as literary works

### Characteristics of Tagore's Drama

His plays typically feature:

**Symbolic Settings**: Settings often symbolic; not merely realistic locations

**Poetic Dialogue**: Characters speak in lyrical, metaphor-rich language rather than naturalistic speech

**Philosophical Themes**: Plays explore ideas—identity, consciousness, love, duty, transcendence

**Internal Focus**: Action often involves internal transformation rather than external events

**Ambiguous Meaning**: Plays often open to multiple interpretations; avoid simple resolutions

**Minimal Plot**: Focus on exploration rather than complex narrative development

**Accessibility**: Despite philosophical depth, plays remain emotionally and dramatically engaging

The play explores:
