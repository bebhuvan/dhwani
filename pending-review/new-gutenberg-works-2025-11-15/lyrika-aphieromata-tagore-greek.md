## Overview

*Λυρικά αφιερώματα* (Lyric Offerings or Spiritual Offerings) is a Greek translation of Rabindranath Tagore's poetry collection, originally composed in Bengali. The 1914 Greek edition presents Tagore's lyrical verses addressing spiritual themes, philosophical reflection, and the dynamics of spiritual dedication and offering. The poems employ clear, accessible language combined with philosophical depth, exploring relationship between individual and universal, personal yearning and transcendent dimensions of experience.

The Greek translation contributed to Tagore's rapid dissemination across European languages following his 1913 Nobel Prize, bringing his contemplative and spiritual poetry to Greek-speaking audiences and demonstrating the universal resonance of his poetic vision.
