Treasury of Truth - Illustrated Dhammapada (Chinese Version) Author: Ven. Weragoda Sarada Maha Thero. Translated by Ms. Ko Guat Hua Illustrations: Mr. P. Wickramanayaka Designs & line drawings: Piyaratna Hewabatage Among the greatest publications in the history of Buddhism; "Treasury of Truth - Illustrated Dhammapada" is a monumental work. Over 900 pages of A4 size. Adorned with 423 illustrations in full-colour, at the rate of one especially commissioned illustration for each stanza. Weighs 4 kg. Included in the text are Pali stanza in Roman Characters - literal translation of stanza - Popular translation - original story out of which stanza stems and commentaries on words, expressions and concepts.
